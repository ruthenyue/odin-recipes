Banana Bread

Description

Bake a delicious loaf of banana bread in one hour!

Easily customize with your favorite add ons such as cinnamon or chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe and soft bananas
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup of white granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter
  • 1 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Prehead your oven to 325 degrees F
  2. Lightly grease loaf pan with coconut oil
  3. Mash bananas and combine with egg, butter, and sugar
  4. Combine flour, salt, and baking soda in a separate bowl before mixing it into banana mixture
  5. Optional: Mix in your favorite add ons such as a dash of cinnamn or 1/4 cup of chocolate chips
  6. Pour batter into greased loaf pan
  7. Bake for 1 hour
  8. You'll know your banana bread is done baking once you can insert a toothpick and have it come out clean
