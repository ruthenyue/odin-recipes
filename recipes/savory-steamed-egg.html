Savory Steamed Egg

Description

A traditional side dish that can be easily made in an instant pot.

Customize this dish by adding your favorite toppings such as scallions or roasted peanuts!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of salted chicken broth
  • green onions
  • sesame oil
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs together
  2. Mix in the chicken broth
  3. Pour the egg and broth mixture through a strainer
  4. Pour strained mixture into a bowl. Cover and make a tight seal with tin foil
  5. Place on a steaming trivet in the instant pot and pressure cook for 6 minutes
  6. Remove tin foil covering and drizzle soy sauce and sesame oil over the steamed egg
  7. Garnish with green onions
