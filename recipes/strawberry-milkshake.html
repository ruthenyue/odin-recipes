Strawberry Milkshake

Description

A sweet and refreshing glass of strawberry milkshake.

Perfect for a hot summer's day!

Ingredients

  • 10 large and washed strawberries
  • 2 1/2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 2 cups oat milk

Instructions

  1. Chop strawberries into large slices
  2. Add the strawberries, ice cream, and milk into a blender
  3. Blend until smooth with a thick consistency
  4. Pour into a glass of your choice and top with a slice of fresh strawberry
